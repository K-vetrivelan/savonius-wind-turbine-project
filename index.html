Savonius Wind Turbine Project

Experimental Investigation of 3D Printed Savonius Wind Turbine

Project Overview

This project explores the design and fabrication of a Savonius wind turbine using additive manufacturing. Multiple blade configurations were developed to study the influence of blade twist angles on turbine performance. Blades were modeled in CAD and fabricated using PLA material through FDM 3D printing technology.
